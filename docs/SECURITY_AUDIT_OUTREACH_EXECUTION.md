Security Audit Outreach Execution (Phase 1)
==========================================

Overview
- Operational steps to perform outreach, collect responses, and select a vendor for the external security audit.

Steps
- Step 1: Finalize shortlist of 3 target firms for outreach (based on domain experience and prior references).
- Step 2: Dispatch initial outreach emails using templates in docs/SECURITY_AUDIT_OUTREACH_EMAILS.md.
- Step 3: Track replies, collect any vendor questions, and issue clarifications.
- Step 4: Issue NDA requests and obtain executed NDAs before sharing code access or SOW details.
- Step 5: Receive proposals and compare against criteria in SECURITY_AUDIT_SOW.md and the backlog.
- Step 6: Shortlist 1–2 vendors for final SOW negotiations; propose kickoff date.
- Step 7: Finalize and sign the SOW; schedule kickoff.

Documentation & Artifacts
- Update SECURITY_AUDIT_BACKLOG.md with ongoing outreach statuses.
- Maintain a concise contact log with vendor names, contacts, dates, and proposal references.

Risks & Mitigations
- Risk: Slow response from vendors. Mitigation: follow-up cadence and alternative vendors.
- Risk: NDA delays. Mitigation: pre-approved NDA draft and standard terms.
