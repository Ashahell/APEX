# OpenFang Adoption Steering Committee — Final Charter (Draft)

Date: 2026-04-15
Status: Draft

1. Purpose
- Formalize governance and decision rights for adopting OpenFang features into APEX.
- Establish clear ownership, accountability, and success criteria for milestone-driven adoption.

2. Scope
- Applies to Hands adoption (e.g., Computer Use MVP), MCP/tool discovery, memory/embeddings, security guardrails, API surface, streaming, and related UI/Adapters.
- Excludes non-OpenFang features outside the adoption plan scope unless explicitly added by the Steering Committee.

3. Authority and Decision Rights
- Steering Committee approves milestones, budget envelopes, and major architectural changes.
- Sub-committees (Hands, MCP, Memory, Security) may propose changes with due process.
- Final arbitration rests with the CTO.

4. Roles and Responsibilities
- Chair: CTO (or delegate)
- Members: Heads of Hands, MCP, Memory, Security, API, UI, and Security Lead
- Secretary: governance admin

5. Deliverables
- Adopted governance cadence and guardrails
- Approved crosswalk documents
- Approved migration plan and onboarding material
- Approved Hands MVP manifest, lifecycle plan, and API surface
- Runbooks for rollouts and incident responses

6. Decision Rules and Process
- Proposals require 2-week feedback window; decisions by majority after discussion.
- Escalations follow the governance escalation path (to CTO).

7. Sign-Off & Versioning
- Signatories: TBD
- Version: v1.0 (draft); revisions to be recorded with date and rationale

8. Review Cycle
- Cadence: quarterly reviews with published minutes; mid-cycle updates as needed

Appendices
- Appendix A: Milestone matrix
- Appendix B: Risk register (initial)
- Appendix C: Communication plan

This charter is intended to be refined and signed by the Steering Committee during the first governance cadence.
