Security Audit Outreach Plan (Phase 1)
====================================

Goal
- Engage 2-3 qualified external security firms to perform the external security audit for APEX v2.0.0 production readiness.

Targets & Criteria for Shortlist
- Firms with proven experience in security audits for Rust/TypeScript codebases, container security, CI/CD security, and cloud deployment hardening.
- Demonstrated ability to deliver actionable remediation plans and re-testing.
- NDA capability and responsive communication.

Outreach & Proposal Timeline
- Day 0: Prepare outreach template, RFP, and NDA; finalize shortlist of target firms.
- Day 1-3: Send initial outreach emails using templates in docs/SECURITY_AUDIT_OUTREACH_EMAILS.md.
- Day 7: Collect vendor questions; issue clarifications if needed.
- Day 14: Receive proposals; evaluate against predefined criteria.
- Day 21: Shortlist 1–2 vendors for final SOW negotiation.
- Day 28: Finalize SOW and schedule kickoff meeting.

Vendor Selection Criteria
- Pricing and timelines that fit our project window
- Clear deliverables: assessment report, remediation plan, evidence pack, retest verification
- Strong references or case studies in similar domains
- Security engineering capabilities (code review, runtime security, CI/CD gates)
- NDA and data handling policies

RFP & NDA Attachments
- SECURITY_AUDIT_SOW.md (updated scope and acceptance criteria)
- SECURITY_AUDIT_KICKOFF.md (kickoff agenda and cadence)

Governance & Approval
- Security Lead to approve final vendor selection and SOW.
- Schedule final kickoff with chosen vendor and internal stakeholders.
