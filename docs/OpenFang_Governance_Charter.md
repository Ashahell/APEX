# OpenFang Adoption Steering Committee Charter

Date: 2026-03-25
Status: Draft

1. Purpose
- Define the scope, principles, and governance model for adopting OpenFang features into APEX.
- Establish clear ownership, accountability, and decision rights for architectural and operational changes related to the OpenFang adoption.

2. Scope
- Covers Hands adoption, MCP, memory/embeddings, security patterns, and API/streaming enhancements associated with the OpenFang integration.
- Depicts how the OpenFang primitives map to APEX components and the plan to migrate features incrementally.

3. Authority and Decision Rights
- Steering Committee decides milestones, budget, and major architectural shifts related to OpenFang adoption.
- Escalates high-risk issues to executive sponsors when needed.

4. Roles and Responsibilities
- Chair: executive sponsor or CTO
- Members: Lead architects for Hands, MCP, Memory, Security; product owner; security lead; program manager
- Scribe/Secretary: governance admin

5. Delivery & Artifacts
- Adoption Charter (this document)
- Governance Cadence documents and minutes
- Crosswalks (APEX ↔ OpenFang)
- Migration plans, onboarding content, and runbooks
- Security guardrails and audit artifacts

6. Decision Process & Meetings
- Decision criteria: risk, impact, effort, alignment with strategy, tests/verification
- Meeting cadence: quarterly with minutes; monthly checkpoints; bi-weekly standups
- Quorum: majority of voting members; escalation path for lack of consensus

7. Sign-off & Amendment
- Amendments require majority approval by the Steering Committee
- Signatories: list of initial signatories (to be added in next version)

This charter is intended to be refined as adoption progresses and needs formal approval.

Addendum: The charter will be reviewed quarterly with governance cadence. Approved signatures to be captured in the final version.
