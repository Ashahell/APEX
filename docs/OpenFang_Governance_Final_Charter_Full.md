OpenFang Adoption Steering Committee — Final Charter (Draft)

Date: 2026-04-15
Status: Draft

1. Purpose
- Define the governance model for adopting OpenFang features into the APEX platform.
- Establish ownership, decision rights, and accountability for architecture, security, and rollout decisions related to OpenFang adoption.
- Provide a clear path for risk management, change control, and sign-off workflows.

2. Scope
- Covers Hands adoption (MVP Hand), MCP/tool registry, memory/embeddings, security guardrails, API and streaming, UI and adapters, and migration/onboarding activities.
- Provides a framework for migrating OpenFang primitives into APEX components with incremental gates.

3. Authority & Decision Rights
- Steering Committee approves milestones, budgets, and major architectural changes.
- Sub-committees (Hands, MCP, Memory, Security) may propose changes with documented rationale and risk assessment.
- Final arbitration rests with the CTO or designated delegate.

4. Roles & Responsibilities
- Chair: TBD (Executive Sponsor)
- Members: Heads of Hands, MCP, Memory, Security, API/Streaming, UI, and Security Lead; Product Owner; Program Manager
- Secretary: Governance Admin

5. Deliverables
- Adopted governance charter (this document)
- Governance cadence documents (cadence + minutes templates)
- Crosswalks (APEX ↔ OpenFang) and migration plan
- Onboarding materials and runbooks for Hands adoption
- Security guardrails and audit artifacts

6. Decision Process & Escalation
- Proposals require a 2-week feedback window; decisions by majority after discussion.
- Escalations follow the governance escalation path to CTO.

7. Sign-Off & Versioning
- Signatories: TBD
- Version: v1.0 (Draft); subsequent revisions will include date and rationale, with signatures in the final version

8. Review Cadence
- Quarterly governance planning with published minutes
- Monthly progress checkpoints for critical streams (Hands, MCP, Memory, Security)
- Bi-weekly engineering standups for blockers

Appendices
- Appendix A: Milestone Matrix (to be populated)
- Appendix B: Risk Register (initial risks to be captured)
- Appendix C: Communication Plan

This charter is intended to be refined and signed by the Steering Committee during the first governance cadence.
