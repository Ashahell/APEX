Security Audit Evaluation Criteria (Phase 1)
===========================================

Purpose
- Provide a transparent rubric to evaluate external security firms for the Phase 1 audit.

Criteria (weighting is indicative)
- Experience and domain fit (Rust/TS, container security, CI/CD hardening) – 25%
- Methodology and deliverables (Assessment, remediation plan, evidence pack, re-test) – 25%
- Access/privacy controls (NDA compliance, data handling, confidentiality) – 15%
- Timelines and staffing (availability, burn rate, team seniority) – 15%
- References and proven outcomes (case studies, similar engagements) – 10%
- Pricing model and total cost (NDA-friendly terms) – 10%

Scoring
- 0-5 per criterion; total score out of 30. Threshold for shortlisting: >= 22.

Process
- Review vendor proposals against this rubric; document rationale for scores and any concerns.
- Use this document to guide NDA requests and kickoff planning.
